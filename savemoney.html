HOW TO SAVE MONEY ON GROCERIES

Did you just have Rugbrød for DINNER? I mean I get it. Living in Denmark is expensive, but this is literally unacceptable. No worries tho, in this article I will show you three ways to save on your groceries AND on your meals.

TooGoodToGo

An app that shows you shops nearby, where you can save food from being thrown away. Last week I got a huge bag full of baked goods from the famous shop with the letter ø for only 39Kr.!

Wefood

WeFood is a shop downtown Århus where you can get groceries that grocery shops are not able to sell (e.g. damaged packaging, almost expired etc.)

Discount brochures

Most of the grocery shops have brochures with weekly discounts. Consider to check them before planning out meals for the week.
